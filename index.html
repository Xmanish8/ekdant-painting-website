Our Waterproofing Process

01
Site Inspection
We assess the extent of leakage and identify all problem areas with professional diagnostic tools.
02
Surface Preparation
Old coatings, loose plaster, and debris are removed. Surface is cleaned and dried thoroughly.
03
Crack & Joint Sealing
All cracks, expansion joints, and vulnerable areas are sealed with high-grade epoxy and sealants.
04
Membrane Application
Liquid waterproof membrane (Dr. Fixit / Roff) is applied in multiple coats for a seamless barrier.
05
Protection Layer
A protective screed or tile layer is applied over the membrane to protect it from UV and physical damage.
06
Water Testing
We conduct a pond test for 24–48 hours to verify complete waterproofing before handing over.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common questions our Nashik customers ask before starting a project.

How much does house painting cost in Nashik?
Painting costs in Nashik typically range from ₹12 to ₹30 per sq ft for interior and ₹18 to ₹35 per sq ft for exterior, depending on paint quality (economy, mid-range, or premium), the condition of existing walls, number of coats required, and accessibility. We provide a detailed written quotation after a free site visit — no surprises.
How long does a complete house painting project take?
Duration depends on the size and complexity. A standard 2BHK interior typically takes 5–7 days. A 3BHK takes 7–10 days. Exterior painting of a bungalow can take 10–15 days. Society projects are completed in phases. We always provide a clear timeline upfront and stick to it.
Which paint brands do you use?
We work with all leading paint brands — Asian Paints, Berger, Nerolac, Indigo, and Birla White. We stock and use authentic materials sourced directly from authorized dealers. Customers can choose their preferred brand and finish; we recommend options based on your budget and requirements.
Do you provide waterproofing warranty?
Yes, our waterproofing treatments using Dr. Fixit systems come with a warranty of up to 5 years, depending on the scope of work and product used. We provide written warranty documentation. In case of any leakage within the warranty period, we return and fix it at no additional cost.
Can I stay at home during the painting work?
Absolutely. We understand the inconvenience of painting work and take every measure to minimize disruption. Our team covers furniture with drop cloths, uses low-odour paints on request, and works systematically room by room so you can continue living in most of your home during the project.
Do you do colour consultation?
Yes! Choosing the right colour is crucial. Our team provides colour consultation using shade cards from Asian Paints, Berger, and Nerolac. We can also use digital colour preview tools to help you visualize your rooms in different shades before committing to a colour scheme.
How do I know if my walls need repainting or just cleaning?
Signs that indicate repainting over cleaning: visible paint peeling or bubbling, deep stains that don't wash off, significant colour fading, cracks in the plaster, or paint older than 5–7 years. Call us for a free inspection — we'll give you an honest assessment and never recommend work that isn't necessary.
What is the difference between interior and exterior paint?
Interior paints are formulated to be washable, low-odour, and easy to apply on smooth indoor surfaces. Exterior paints contain special binders and additives to withstand UV rays, rain, dust, temperature fluctuations, and pollution. Using the wrong paint type dramatically reduces longevity — we ensure the right paint for every surface.
Do you handle commercial and industrial painting projects?
Yes, we have extensive experience in commercial, industrial, and institutional painting. We've worked on offices, factories, warehouses, hospitals, schools, and housing societies across Nashik. Industrial painting uses specialized coatings for metal surfaces, high-traffic areas, and chemical-resistant finishes where required.
How do I prepare my home before the painters arrive?
We handle most of the preparation ourselves. We appreciate if you can: remove fragile decorative items and artwork, clear space near walls for our drop cloths, disconnect electrical appliances from sockets near walls, and ensure pets are kept safe. Our team covers furniture, floors, and fixtures before any painting begins.
What is wall putty and why is it important?
Wall putty is a fine white powder mixed with water and applied to walls before painting to create a smooth, even base. It fills minor imperfections, nail holes, and surface irregularities. Without proper putty work, even the most expensive paint will look uneven. It's a non-negotiable step in our painting process for any premium finish.
Do you offer EMI or payment in installments?
We typically collect payment in three stages: 30% advance upon confirmation, 40% after surface preparation and primer coat, and the remaining 30% upon project completion. For large society or commercial projects, we can structure a custom payment plan. We accept cash, bank transfer, UPI, and cheque.
Is terrace waterproofing necessary even if there's no current leakage?
Preventive waterproofing is always better and cheaper than reactive repairs. Once a terrace starts leaking, water has likely already penetrated the slab, causing invisible damage to reinforcement rods and concrete. Waterproofing a dry terrace costs a fraction of repairing a water-damaged structure. We recommend waterproofing for all terraces older than 5 years.
Do you service areas outside Nashik city?
Our primary service area is Nashik city and its surrounding areas including Gangapur Road, Panchavati, Nashik Road, Satpur, Ambad, Cidco, College Road, Dwarka, and Indira Nagar. For larger projects in Nashik district such as Sinnar, Igatpuri, and Trimbakeshwar, we can accommodate with prior discussion.
How do you ensure quality control throughout the project?
Mr. Dattu Marathe or a senior supervisor personally oversees every project. We follow a strict 8-step quality checklist and document each stage. Customers can visit the site at any time. We conduct thorough inspections before and after every coat, and only release the final payment request after the customer has done a walk-through and is completely satisfied.
